SMYRT

My main way of giving back to the community has consisted of taking part in an organization at St. Mary's Catholic Center called SMYRT, which stands for St. Mary's Youth Retreat Team. Within SMYRT, I have been formed by leadership and speakers to prepare me and my peers for retreats. I have staffed a few retreats and will be a retreat leader next semester. We put on multiple retreats every semester at locations about 2 or less hours away from College Station, TX. The retreats are Catholic retreats held for middle or high schoolers. They serve to help deepen the faith of the youth so that they can make an impact on the world.

Connect

Another way I have given back to my community is serving on staff for the St. Mary's Connect retreat. This is a retreat held every year to welcome new students to College Station, especially the St. Mary's community. It provides the students with fellowship, opportunities to grow in their faith, and ways to get involved at St. Mary's. I attended Connect as a freshman, and have gotten the opportunity to run slides for the retreat the past two years, helping the retreat to run smoothly.
